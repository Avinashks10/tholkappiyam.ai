About Tholkappiyam AI

Tholkappiyam AI is an initiative to modernize the study and exploration of ancient Tamil grammar through AI-powered search and interaction.

This project was started by Avinash K S to combine the timeless knowledge of Tholkappiyam with modern design and usability.

We are continuously working to enhance features and make it a complete AI companion for Tamil literature enthusiasts.
